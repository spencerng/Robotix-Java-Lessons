    Raider Robotix Scouting

    Welcome to the hub for Raider Robotix Scouting! Here you'll find the latest info from the Scouting Subteam, from app programming and scout training resources, to scouting rotations and standings for the current season. Of course, click here to visit the team website.

    This site will be hosted on the team's GitHub repository in the future.
